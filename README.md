A simple web app for CityFitness members to register their guests for gym access at CityFitness Invercargill.

## How to Use

### Getting Started
1. Open the app on your phone or computer
2. Fill in your member information (saved for future use)
3. Enter your guest's details
4. Submit the form

### Install on Your Phone (Optional)
1. Open the app in your mobile browser (Chrome, Safari, etc.)
2. Look for "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App" option
3. Install it as an app on your device for easy access

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **Enter Member Information** (saved for future use):
   - Your first and last name
   - Your member key tag number

2. **Enter Guest Information**:
   - Guest's first and last name  
   - Guest's email address
   - Guest's phone number

3. **Review Terms**: Check the terms and conditions box

4. **Submit**: Tap "Submit Guest Pass" to send the request
